# Adding Plain Text to DocsAI
Select **Text** from the available sources and proceed.

Use text to add smaller details  to chatbot, instead of adding them to your website or files.
Text can also be used to suggest answers to certain questions, where adding answers to your website or files is unnecessary.

![text](/docs/text.jpeg)

### Sync plain text

You can `edit` or `delete` the plain text at the `Documents` page
