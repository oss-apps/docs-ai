# Add Notion as a knowledge basae for Chatbot

Select **Notion** from the available sources and proceed.

### Connecting to notion
Click `Connect Notion` to open notion ,  select the pages you want to train. 
You will be redirected to DocsAI automatically.

![notion](/docs/notion.webp)

### Train some notion pages
You can skip some notion pages if you don't want to train now using the `delete` icon or you can update the notion access itself.

Training may take some time, depending on the amount of content you have provided. Feel free to leave this page and check back in a few minutes.
