# Organization

An organization can hold multiple projects and each project refers to a chatbot. You can be part of
other organisation and get access to that projects.

Although adding users to your organization is free, the feature is not yet visible in DocsAI. However, you can email us hey@docsai.app and we will add users to your organization for you.

An organization selects a subscription plan that benefits all of its members.
