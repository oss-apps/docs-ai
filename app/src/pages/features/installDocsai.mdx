# Install Docs AI on your devices

## Desktop 

### Chromium based browsers

**Go to DocsAI website** and  **Install DocsAI web app**: Click on the **`Install`** button. The browser will prompt you to confirm the installation. Click on **`Install`** button to proceed with the installation. 
Now DocsAI will be avalaible at your start menu.

### Safari

From the menu bar, choose File > Add to Dock or click the Share button  <IconShare className="inline w-5 h-5 mr-2" />supplied for Image in the Safari toolbar, then choose **Add to Dock**.
